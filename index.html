About ITconference

Welcome to the ITconference 2023, where innovation meets inspiration! The ITconference is a premier gathering of tech enthusiasts, developers, and design aficionados from around the globe. Immerse yourself in a world of cutting-edge technologies, interactive workshops, and engaging discussions. Discover the latest trends in frontend technologies, UX design, and responsive design that are shaping the digital landscape.
Mark your calendars for the 8th to 10th of December 2023! The ITconference will take place in the vibrant city of Oslo, Norway. Embrace the winter wonderland of Oslo as you delve into three days of enriching experiences. Our carefully curated venue provides the perfect backdrop for networking, learning, and forging lasting connections with fellow tech enthusiasts.
At the ITconference 2023, the spotlight is on frontend technologies, UX design, and responsive design. Dive deep into the intricacies of crafting seamless user experiences, explore the latest advancements in frontend development, and unlock the secrets of creating designs that adapt to any device. Our lineup of expert speakers and hands-on workshops will equip you with the skills and insights needed to stay ahead in the ever-evolving world of IT. Join us on this journey of learning, collaboration, and innovation!
